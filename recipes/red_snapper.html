Pan-Seared Red Snapper Fillet

Description

This Pan-Seared Red Snapper features flaky, tender white fish with a perfectly crisp, golden-brown skin seasoned with a blend of aromatic spices. The fillets are quickly seared in a hot skillet to lock in moisture and then finished with a vibrant butter sauce infused with fresh lemon and garlic. It is a simple yet elegant seafood dish that highlights the natural sweetness of the snapper, making it a versatile choice for a quick weeknight dinner or a more formal gathering.

Ingredients

  • 2 Red Snapper fillets (skin-on)
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon onion powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried oregano
  • Salt and black pepper to taste
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons unsalted butter
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon fresh lemon juice
  • Fresh parsley, chopped (for garnish)
  • Lemon wedges (for serving)

Steps

  1. Prepare the Fish: Pat the red snapper fillets completely dry with paper towels to ensure the skin gets crispy. Use a sharp knife to make 2–3 shallow scores on the skin side of each fillet to prevent curling during cooking.
  2. Season: In a small bowl, mix the paprika, garlic powder, onion powder, oregano, salt, and pepper. Rub the seasoning blend evenly over both sides of the fillets.
  3. Sear the Skin Side: Heat the olive oil in a large non-stick or cast-iron skillet over medium-high heat. Once the oil is shimmering, place the fillets in the pan, skin-side down. Press down gently with a spatula for a few seconds to ensure even contact.
  4. Cook: Sear the skin side for about 3–4 minutes without moving the fish, until the skin is golden and crispy.
  5. Flip and Finish: Carefully flip the fillets over. Add the butter and minced garlic to the skillet. Cook the flesh side for another 2–3 minutes, spooning the melting garlic butter over the fish as it finishes cooking.
  6. Deglaze: Drizzle the fresh lemon juice over the fillets and remove the skillet from the heat.
  7. Serve: Garnish with chopped fresh parsley and serve immediately with extra lemon wedges on the side.
