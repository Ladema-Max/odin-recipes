Creamy Tuscan Chicken with Orzo

Description

This creamy Tuscan chicken with orzo is the ultimate comfort meal. Chicken breasts are simmered in a rich and creamy Parmesan cheese sauce with sun-dried tomatoes, spinach, and garlic and served with orzo

Ingredients

  • 1 Cup Orzo
  • 2 Tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 1/2 pound skinless, boneless chicken breast, cut into 1-inch pieces
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1/2 cup chicken broth
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon Italian seasoning
  • 3/4 teaspoon Kosher salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon freshly ground black pepper
  • 1, 5-ounce box baby spinach
  • 1/2 cup chopped sun-dried tomatoes
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil
  2. Cook Orzo in boiling water
  3. Stir ocassionally untill tender yet firm to the bite, for about 11 minutes
  4. Heat olive oil in large skillet over medium-high heat
  5. Cook chicken until browned, 3 to 5 minutes per side
  6. Remove and set aside
  7. Add heavy cream, chicken broth, garlic powder, Italian seasoning,salt and pepper to the same skillet
  8. Whisk sauce over medium-high heat until it thickens for about 5 minutes
  9. REturn chicken to skillet and cook for about 2 minutes
  10. Add spinach, sun-dried tomatoes and Parmesan cheese
  11. Simmer until spinach starts to wilt, about 1 minute
  12. Stir in cooked Orzo
  13. Cook for 1 minute
  14. Remove chicken from heat
  15. Let stand until thickened
